Congratulations on being part of this elite group of Data Scientists in Accenture

Master Data Science Challenges is a series of Data Science problems that participants would try to solve. Each challenge represents a typical client Data Science problem. Participants compete as teams to build the "Best Solution" based on a training dataset.

The solution modeling efficacy is determined based on performance of the solution on test dataset(s). The metric used would be predetermined for each challenge. Also, each team is required to submit an Executive Summary(maximum of two pages) explaining the solution. Each participant would accumulate Challenge Points based upon the above submissions

The team/ individual with maximum Challenge Points across the challenges would be the winner.

No Hardware/ common computation environment will be provided for the competition. Participants are expected to use their Accenture provided computing resources.

RULES AT A GLANCE:

Participate as teams. Any team member can submit and submission will reflect on the team’s score.

Teams can submit multiple entries for modelling results. The "best" score would be used to allocate Challenge Points. A daily and overall cap may be introduced if gaming is suspected.

Use of any External data is not permitted.

Any modelling technique can be leveraged.

ONLY the last Executive Summary submission before the deadline would be evaluated.

Subjective evaluation done by SME’s are final

Points allocation may be finetuned in order to achieve the outcome desired by Accenture.


How can I earn Challenge Points?

Each challenge provides an opportunity to earn a maximum of 100 Challenge Points. Challenge Points are allocated for modeling efficacy as well as executive summary. All members of a team earn same Challenge Points.

MDSC01: Predicting Credit Card Fraud

Credit card fraud transactions is one of biggest issues faced by consumers and credit card issuers. Estimated world-wide loss due to fraud is USD 28.65 Billion* in 2019 to credit card issuers. A key part of fraud mitigation is fraud detection by leveraging sophisticated analytics in identifying fraudulent transaction on real time basis Challenge is to identify potential fraudulent credit card transaction. In MDSC01, you will build a binary classification model based on Train dataset and then use it to predict possibly fraudulent transactions on Test dataset.
